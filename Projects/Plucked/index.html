Overview

The name reflects how our brand 'plucks' chickens and from their life sentence in poultry farms to go to happier homes. This concept is reflected in our motion, illustrative style, and tone of voice. Plucked is unapologetically bold, which is achieved through the brand's tone of voice to raise awareness and stand against the mistreatment of chickens in the poultry industry.

Simultaneously, the brand needed to be friendly, appealing to families, and promoting a positive outlook on what is otherwise a bleak situation. We accomplished this through the illustrated and 3D modelled character Pluck, our quirky chicken, with bouncy animations and fun expressions.
