Kellogg's

A bold advertising campaign for Kellogg's

A Kellogg’s for mental health campaign to highlight the benefits of of breakfast in the morning. By ignoring and not replenishing your glucose levels in the morning (skipping breakfast) you will end up feeling overly hungry, irritable and fatigued. We developed this advertising campaign video to push the effects of a healthy bowl of cornflakes in the morning. A fast and fun paced animation, created from the bottom up, featuring modelled and rigged 3D characters.
