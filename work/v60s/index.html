V60's

We created a title sequence for a old school vintage cars TV series.

Using CGI, type design, and vector motion, we created a cinematic intro for V60's, a vintage motorsport and antique car show. Opting to use 3D CGI rather than 2D assets was an easy choice, we had detailed models of cars to use, giving us fine control over the angles and composition of the sequence. The priceless and exotic vehicles featured in V60's needed to be represented by something fine, and masterful; hence the choice of car for the shots - the 1960s Mercedes 300 SL. The rest of the video was designed in 2D, with a focus on the colour grading, and motion type.
